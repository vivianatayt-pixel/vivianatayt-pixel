PROFILE

Jeon Jungkook, born on September 1, 1997, in Busan, South Korea, is best known as the youngest member and main vocalist of the global sensation BTS. Debuting under BigHit Entertainment in 2013, Jungkook quickly gained recognition for his versatile talents, from his powerful vocals and sharp dance skills to his natural stage charisma. Often referred to as the groups Golden Maknae, he embodies the ideal of an all-rounder artist, capable of seamlessly blending technical mastery with emotional depth in every performance.

Beyond his role in BTS, Jungkook has carved a distinct identity as a solo artist. His singles such as Euphoria, Still With You, and Seven showcase his artistic growth and vocal versatility, blending genres like pop, R&B, and electronic music. His solo debut album GOLDEN (2023) solidified his global presence, topping charts worldwide and setting streaming records. Each project reflects Jungkooks evolving artistry, one thats refined, deeply personal, yet universally resonant.

Outside music, Jungkooks influence extends into fashion, fitness, and pop culture. Known for his humble and introspective personality, he balances superstardom with a down to earth relatability that endears him to millions. Whether performing on grand stages or sharing quiet moments with fans online, Jungkook embodies a rare mix of discipline, creativity, and authenticity, qualities that continue to define his journey as one of the most influential artists of his generation.
